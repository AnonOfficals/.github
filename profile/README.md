<h2>Mission</h2>
<p>At <strong>Anon Open Source</strong>, our mission is to build a global community of developers and cybersecurity enthusiasts. We create cutting-edge open-source tools, provide reliable security solutions, and promote ethical hacking practices. Our goal is to make technology safer, more accessible, and innovative for everyone.</p>
